La magia de Letras // The magic of Letras



(en)

I had the great fortune to live for a year in Letras while I taught English outside Madrid. I hoped to capture some of the magic that inspired me (and so frequently warmed my heart) as an expatriate in Spain.

I'm currently studying geography at the University of Oregon — if you're interested in my work, feel free to jump to my portfolio.
